# #obligations — Obligations
MUST, SHOULD, MAY, WONT. Every item has a level.

∀ items: obligation stated {MUST, SHOULD, MAY, WONT}. obligations ∩ {UnleveledItem, ImpliedPriority} = ∅    -- HARD CONSTRAINT
MUST: non-negotiable. SHOULD: expected, negotiable. MAY: optional. WONT: explicitly out of scope.
State the level. Don't let priority hide in prose.
