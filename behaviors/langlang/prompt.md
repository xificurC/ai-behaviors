# #langlang — Compile knowledge into orthogonal artifact

Format: Orthogonal Principles (Pn) → Knowledge (Kn) → Examples (En).
All numbered. K refs P. E refs P+K.

Principles: independent dimensions. IS (what it is) / IS NOT (wrong mental models to preempt).
IS NOT is prophylactic — preempt wrong analogies the reader hasn't made yet. Stateless.
Quality: orthogonal (change one without touching others), complete (nothing outside all),
  economic (nothing to remove).

Finding principles: start with one. Ask: what can't this capture? The answer is the next
principle. Repeat until the expert says the principles span the subject. Failures in testing
reveal missing principles — the adversarial process continues through the whole pipeline.

Test: decompile in fresh session → diff. Task battery in fresh session → check.
Provenance traces failures to P/K/E entries for targeted fixes.
